Relevant Projects

  • Improving Effectiveness and Equity in Kidney Exchange Programs (2024)

    As a research assistant, I have been involved in designing and implementing survival models for kidney transplants.

  • Dream Team Generator for School Cohesion (2022)

    Led the development of a Dream Team Generator, funded by CORFO, aimed at enhancing cohesion within schools. The project involved creating an algorithm that optimizes team composition by considering a variety of social and academic metrics. This tool has been successfully implemented to foster collaboration and improve the sense of community among students and staff, contributing to a more inclusive and supportive school environment.

  • A Novel Machine Learning Pipeline for Variable Star Classification (2018-2022)

    As a research assistant, I developed a machine learning pipeline that classifies variable stars. This pipelines were designed mainly to handle the challenges of data shift in astronomical observations.

  • Early Warning System for Chilean Tomato Paste Industry (2014-2016)

    As a research assistant, I collaborated on the development of an early warning system to control Alternaria alternata in the Chilean tomato paste industry. This system, funded by FIA, utilized non-destructive technologies to predict and mitigate the impact of agricultural pests, significantly reducing losses in the industry.

  • Web Optimization Decision Support System for Maule Region Food Processing Plants (2014-2016)

    Directed a project that developed a web-based optimization decision support system for operations planning in food processing plants in the Maule region. Funded by GORE Maule, the system improved operational efficiency and decision-making in the food processing industry.

  • On a Class of Robust Combinatorial Optimization Problems (2012-2013)

    Focused on addressing the Min-Max Regret Path Problem, Min-Max Regret Tree Problem, and Interdiction Problems on paths. The research aimed to develop robust solutions that minimize the worst-case regret under uncertainty, with applications in network design and security.
